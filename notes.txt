Animation and Forms
--------------------------------

Sub Properties for animation 

1 animation-name: Tells the browsers what CSS rule to use 
2: animation-duration: How much time it takes for one cycle to complete 
3: animation-timing-function: acceleration curve of the animation 
4: animation-iteration-count: how many times the animation should be performed
5: Allows us to combine name, duration and iteration count
